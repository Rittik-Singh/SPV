LIBRARY

The SPV library is a student's delight. The spacious library is stacked with a number of books, Journals and periodicals. The library has silently grown its collection over the past years and is enriched with books of all streams useful for the students. There are over 15000 books to cater to the students across all the streams.

  

The Computer Laboratory is well-equipped with number of upgraded computers. The Computer faculties take special care of students of all classes especially of those pursuing the courses of the Higher Secondary Syllabus.


  

The school provides the students with excellent faculties for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Nutrition and Geography. The spancious laboratories, those which have grown for years, are adorned with all the equipments and appliances necessary for the concerned subjects. Unlike many other organisations, the school supplies the students with various facilities so that they can individually handle the equipments in favour of the enrichment in the field of academic career.
