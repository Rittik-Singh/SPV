Swami Pranabanda Vidyapith, a unit of the prestigeous and internationally recognised Bharat Sevashram Sangha, has truly emerged as the foremost centre of academics and culture in the Karunamoyee Kudghat belt of Tollygunge area under the constant monitoring and able tutelage of a highly dedicated teaching fraternity. Resuming its journey in the year 1990 with the aim of making worthy nation-builders, the institute has taken long strides in establishing itself as the most preferred and automatic choice of the students and guardians of the nearby and far-off areas through its highly satisfactory result on a consistent basis in both academic and cultural arenas.

Swami Paranabananda Vidyapith would surely encourage and inspire a learner to come out of the shells of materialistic confinements and soar high in the horizon of infinite peace and unbound joy on the wings of self realization and self confidence to scale the heights of optimum excellence and ultimate success.
